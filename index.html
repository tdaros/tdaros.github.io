About Me

I am an Analog IC Design Engineer at the International Iberian Nanotechnology Laboratory (INL), specializing in integrated circuits for sensor applications. Concurrently, I am a PhD candidate at the Federal University of Santa Catarina (UFSC), with research on Floating Gate Dosimeters (expected completion: Aug 2025).

With a passion for the complete ASIC design cycle—from system modeling to characterization—I enjoy tackling challenges in ultra-low-voltage design and developing innovative microelectronic solutions.
